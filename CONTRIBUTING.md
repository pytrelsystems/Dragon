 # Contributing to Dragon

Dragon is owned and governed by Pytrel Systems LLC.

We welcome engagement from individuals and teams working on:
- Deterministic systems
- Agent governance
- Auditable automation
- Operational AI with clear authority boundaries

## How to Engage

- Open an Issue to discuss ideas, architecture, or alignment.
- Do not submit pull requests without prior discussion.
- Dragon is not a community sandbox.

## Philosophy

We value:
- Clear thinking
- Restraint
- Evidence over hype
- Progress shared honestly

If that resonates, you are in the right place.
